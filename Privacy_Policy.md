# Privacy Policy for BoxMaster

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to **BoxMaster** ("we," "our," or "us"), developed by **AZ Develops**. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application ("App").

BoxMaster is designed as a **fully offline game**. We prioritize user privacy by ensuring that no personal data is collected, transmitted, or processed externally.

---

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or non-personal information.**

Specifically, BoxMaster does NOT collect:
- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Device identifiers or IP addresses
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Advertising identifiers
- Payment or financial data

The App operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

---

## Local Data Storage

BoxMaster stores limited data **locally on your device only** to enhance gameplay and user experience. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

### Game Preferences

- Player names (local profiles only)
- Game mode settings
- Sound and vibration preferences

### Game Statistics

- Total games played
- Wins, losses, and draws
- Match history and performance records

### Data Deletion

You can remove all locally stored data by:
- Uninstalling the App, or
- Clearing App storage via:  
  **Settings → Apps → BoxMaster → Storage → Clear Data**

---

## Third-Party Services

**BoxMaster does not use any third-party services.**

This includes:
- No advertising networks
- No analytics platforms
- No cloud storage providers
- No authentication services
- No online multiplayer services
- No external APIs or SDKs

All functionality runs entirely on your device.

---

## Internet and Backend Services

BoxMaster:
- Does **not** require an internet connection
- Does **not** connect to any servers
- Does **not** transmit game data or device data
- Does **not** use cloud-based features

The App is fully functional in offline mode.

---

## Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

Since BoxMaster does **not process personal data**, no legal basis for data processing is required under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

All data remains under the user's direct control on their own device.

---

## Permissions

BoxMaster requests the following device permission:

- **VIBRATE**  
  Used solely to provide optional haptic feedback during gameplay.

This permission is not used for data collection, tracking, or monitoring.

---

## Children’s Privacy

BoxMaster is suitable for a **general audience**, including children under the age of 13.

We do **not collect personal data** from any users, including children. As a result, the App complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design.

---

## Data Security

We do not store any user data on external servers.

All locally stored data is protected by your device’s built-in operating system security mechanisms. We recommend:
- Keeping your device software up to date
- Using device-level security features such as screen locks and encryption

- Access your locally stored App data
- Delete App data by clearing storage or uninstalling the App
- Contact the developer regarding privacy concerns

Since no personal data is collected or stored externally, requests for data access or portability are not applicable.
